About Us

Revolutionizing Respiratory Diagnostics

Our project focuses on the automatic detection and classification of respiratory diseases using advanced deep learning techniques. By leveraging state-of-the-art methods, we aim to address the global health burden posed by respiratory conditions like COPD and pneumonia, facilitating early diagnosis and timely interventions.

To develop an efficient, automated diagnostic tool capable of accurately identifying respiratory diseases, enhancing healthcare outcomes worldwide.

  • Leveraging deep learning for enhanced accuracy
  • Addressing global health challenges
  • Promoting early detection and timely treatment
  • Developing robust and lightweight solutions
